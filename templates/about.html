About Me

I am a cockatiel.

As you might imagine, I have a ghostwriter.

I love peanuts! There, I said it. I'm a bird, I eat nuts, all kinds of nuts. But the best are peanuts, and I go for the best.

I was born in central Minnesota, and I came to live with my humans when I was eight weeks old. I could tell they weren't quite sure what they got (nor was I), but they soon came around to my kind of thinking. I began their training a couple of weeks after I got here, I had decided that patience was the better choice and I gave them a little time to adjust before launching into the vitally important Human Training Program (HTP). The HTP is a Bird-Certified program of the International Bird Coalition (IBC) that provides assurances that our humans are bird-rated and suitable for rearing and caring for pet birds.

After a slow start my humans are coming around to the HTP approach, which includes freedom (of course!), but also oodles of toys, constant food, hugs and scratches, and, of course, the ever-present poop (Python Object-Oriented Programming). Freedom is essential for the positive development of humans. I let them wander about the house and yard as they wish. It's important to me for them to be happy and carefree as they develop into proper 'pet-owners', and to ensure they continue to believe they own a pet. I'm happy to report that my humans are responding well to the HTP (and that I remain in charge).

Certified: International Bird Coalition
